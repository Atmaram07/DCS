Client Profile

Background

Our client, a top-tier Indian steel manufacturer, operates a legacy steel plant with 6 million tonnes annual production capacity. Despite its size and experience, the company faced operational limitations threatening scalability and profitability in the modern era, including:

  • Unpredictable equipment failures and unplanned downtime
  • Inefficient manual data capture across SCADA, MES, and ERP layers
  • High rework and rejection rates due to poor inline quality control
  • Energy inefficiencies across furnace and rolling operations
  • No real-time insights across units or data standardization

The management’s vision was clear:

“We want this plant to become a world-class Smart Factory with predictive intelligence, automation, and real-time control — not just for performance but for global competitiveness.”

Why DCS Was Chosen

Dodong Consulting Services (DCS) was selected after a multi-round evaluation over global system integrators and consultants, for one key reason:

We not only deploy technology — we engineer measurable transformation across people, process, and platforms.

DCS Transformation Strategy: “SMART-FAB” Framework

DCS deployed its proprietary 5-stage SMART-FAB framework to transform the plant into a Smart Industry 4.0 Lighthouse Factory:

  • S – Sensorization & IoT Infrastructure:
    • Installed 2,000+ IIoT-grade sensors across blast furnaces, continuous casting, rolling, and packing lines
    • Integrated with legacy PLCs via MQTT & OPC UA protocols
    • Established real-time data lake with 98.6% asset visibility
    • ✅ Impact: Full digital footprint of core plant processes captured within 8 weeks
  • M – Machine Learning & Predictive Intelligence:
    • Built live anomaly detection models (bearing wear, furnace drift, motor vibration)
    • Developed predictive maintenance triggers reducing MTTR and false alarms
    • Used ML to predict quality deviations, minimizing rejects during hot-rolling
    • ✅ Impact:
      • Unplanned downtime reduced by 41%
      • Bearing-related failure incidents cut from 21/year to 8/year
      • Inline product quality success rate improved to 98.3%
  • A – Automation, Robotics & AR:
    • Introduced robotic coil handling and automated slag removal arms
    • Enabled AR-guided maintenance for field teams using smart glasses
    • Launched AGVs (automated guided vehicles) for billet transport inside the plant
    • ✅ Impact:
      • Operator productivity improved by 29%
      • Manual fatigue and error incidents dropped by 44%
      • Maintenance resolution time reduced by 34%
  • R – Real-Time KPI Dashboards:
    • Rolled out CommandOps™ Visual Cockpit Dashboards across plant heads
    • Unified metrics: OEE, scrap %, energy per ton, predictive downtime alerts
    • Enabled shift-wise performance benchmarking & deviation alerts
    • ✅ Impact:
      • First-pass yield improved from 91% to 98.3%
      • Energy wastage reduced by 21%
      • Decision latency (average issue-to-action time) dropped by 48%
  • T – Total Systems Integration:
    • Integrated SCADA, MES, ERP, and legacy data historians into a single digital layer
    • Built APIs to sync with procurement, inventory, and distribution
    • Developed a Digital Twin model for real-time scenario simulation and planning
    • ✅ Impact:
      • Seamless visibility across production, supply chain, and maintenance
      • Faster monthly closing and real-time operational reconciliation
      • Roadmap built to replicate the model at two other national plants
