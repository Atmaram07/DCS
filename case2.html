Empowering Digital Transformation and Growth in Education Technology


How Dodong Consultancy Services Catalyzed User Growth, Retention, and Operational Excellence for a Leading EdTech Innovator

Client Profile

  • Industry: Education Technology (K-12, Test Preparation, Career Development)
  • Geography: India & Middle East
  • User Base at Engagement Start: ~1.3 Million Active Learners
  • Engagement Duration: 12 Months
  • Engagement Type: Digital transformation, growth strategy, and technology modernization

Executive Summary

The education technology landscape is rapidly evolving, with learners demanding personalized, engaging, and accessible learning experiences.

Our client—a leading EdTech platform—was encountering scalability challenges, suboptimal learner retention, limited regional reach, and an inability to harness learner data for actionable insights.

Dodong Consultancy Services (DCS) partnered with this organization to drive a comprehensive transformation using our proprietary LEARN-X™ Framework, focused on personalization, technology modernization, regionalization, and data intelligence. Over 12 months, DCS delivered remarkable outcomes that established our client as a market leader and accelerated their growth trajectory.

Challenges

Challenge Details
Scalability & Platform Stability Legacy monolithic infrastructure caused frequent downtime and poor user experience during peak periods.
Low Learner Retention Retention rates were below industry benchmarks, primarily due to non-adaptive content and limited engagement tactics.
Restricted Regional Penetration Limited multi-language support and localized content restricted access to Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.
Insufficient Analytics & Insights Absence of real-time, actionable learner data hindered personalized interventions and strategic decisions.
Competitive Market Pressures Emerging competitors were gaining traction with adaptive learning technologies and better user experiences.

DCS Solution Framework: LEARN-X™

Our approach employed the LEARN-X™ Framework, a five-pillar strategy tailored for EdTech growth and operational excellence:

Pillar Strategy & Execution
1. Learner Segmentation & Behavioral Intelligence Developed AI-powered learner personas and behavior analytics to deliver personalized experiences.
2. Engagement Personalization & Adaptive Learning Journeys Implemented dynamic content sequencing, gamification elements, and personalized learning paths to increase engagement.
3. Architecture Modernization & Scalability Migrated to a microservices-based, cloud-native platform enabling high availability and seamless scaling.
4. Regionalization & Localization Rolled out multi-language support and culturally contextualized learning materials for wider reach.
5. Learning Analytics & Predictive Insights Built a centralized analytics dashboard to monitor learner progress, predict dropout risk, and optimize content delivery.

Implementation Highlights

  • Cloud Migration: Transitioned platform to AWS with Kubernetes orchestration, improving uptime from 88% to 99.9%.
  • AI-Driven Personalization: Developed machine learning models that forecast learner disengagement, enabling proactive interventions.
  • Localization: Launched 7 regional languages, enhancing accessibility and adoption in underserved markets.
  • Agile Collaboration: Iterative delivery cycles ensured rapid deployment and continuous improvement aligned with client goals.
  • Comprehensive Analytics: Real-time dashboards empowered leadership with data to inform content strategy and marketing efforts.
