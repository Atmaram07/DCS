Client Profile

  • Client: Confidential – Mid-stage Lifestyle Brand
  • Sector: E-commerce & Direct-to-Consumer (D2C)
  • Location: India (Scaling Globally)
  • Engagement Duration: 12 months
  • Annual Revenue (Start): ₹18 Cr ($2.2M)
  • Annual Revenue (Post): ₹75 Cr+ ($9M+)
  • Total ROI on DCS Engagement: 7.3x

Background

A fast-growing lifestyle brand offering sustainable apparel and wellness products approached DCS with a bold ambition:

“We want to be India’s first globally loved sustainable D2C brand without burning capital like the others.”

Despite strong early traction, they faced challenges:

  • Plateaued revenue after initial virality
  • Rising Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC)
  • Fragmented brand story across channels
  • Logistic inefficiencies and high RTOs
  • Low repeat purchase rate (14%)
  • Failed attempts at international expansion

Why DCS Was Chosen

DCS was selected for its ability to drive holistic transformation beyond just performance marketing:

We don’t just grow brands — we build scalable ecosystems across brand, tech, and growth funnels.

DCS Transformation Strategy

  • Brand System Restructuring:
    • Created a unified “Brand OS”
    • Mission-led storytelling across touchpoints
    • 3.4x higher brand recall
  • Performance Growth & Funnel Optimization:
    • Persona micro-segmentation + Zero-Waste Funnel™
    • 1-click checkout, real-time triggers, UGC integration
    • CVR ↑ 41%, CAC ↓ 38%, ROAS ↑ 142%
  • Customer Retention & Loyalty:
    • Predictive LTV analytics + cohort marketing
    • VIP loyalty program, Discord/WhatsApp community
    • Repeat rate ↑ from 14% to 39%
  • International Scaling:
    • US market entry with localized campaigns
    • $2.3M ARR from US in Year 1, CSAT: 94%
  • Backend & Ops Infrastructure:
    • Unified ERP, predictive RTO engine, automated returns
    • RTO dropped from 27% → 9%, logistics cost ↓ 21%
