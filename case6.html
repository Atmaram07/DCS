Client Profile

  • Industry: Agritech & Smart Farming
  • Geography: Uttar Pradesh, India
  • Client Type: Simulated cooperative of 5,000 wheat & horticulture farmers
  • Engagement Scope: AI, IoT, satellite integration, credit enablement, market linkage
  • Model Type: Representative simulation based on real agritech benchmarks

Executive Summary

Dodong Consulting Services (DCS) designed a simulated AI- and IoT-powered smart farming solution tailored to India's fragmented agrarian landscape. Using precision agriculture, satellite analytics, and market-linked forecasting, the model demonstrated:

  • Up to 35% yield increase
  • 28% reduction in agri-waste

The simulation provides tangible benefits to farmers, agritech startups, and institutional agri-lenders.

Challenges

  • Rain-dependent farming and severe water stress
  • Middlemen reducing farmer profits by 20–40%
  • Post-harvest losses due to poor cold chains
  • Lack of real-time price and weather information

Despite these barriers, India is seeing the rise of over 500 agritech startups ready to transform rural incomes.

DCS Solution: AGRI-INTEL™ Framework

DCS implemented a four-layered digital transformation model for the simulation:

  • Soil & Crop AI Engine
    • Soil sensors (moisture, pH, NPK, EC)
    • TensorFlow LSTM models for nutrient prediction
    • Result: 19% input efficiency, 33% less water use
  • Market Intelligence & Predictive Pricing
    • Price prediction using Prophet & XGBoost
    • Sell alerts via WhatsApp
    • Result: 16% better price realization
  • IoT + Satellite-integrated Crop Monitoring
    • Sentinel-2 & Planet Labs with NDVI, EVI health indices
    • Farmer dashboard with disease/pest alerts
    • Result: 22% lower crop loss
  • Smart Lending via Alternate Data
    • ML-based credit scores using agri-IoT, transaction history
    • Result: 64% credit eligibility, 48-hr loan disbursal

Implementation Highlights

  • Soil IoT Rollout: 3 crop zones sensorized with wireless sync
  • Pricing Alerts: Market signals delivered via WhatsApp to 5,000 farmers
  • Health Dashboard: Real-time pest/disease alerts on mobile with geo-marked visuals
  • Credit API: Secure API gateway built to connect with NBFCs and digital lenders
